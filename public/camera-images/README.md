# Camera Images

Place your Cascina Leone camera images here.

These images will be:
- Read by the daily email generator
- Transformed into oil paintings by Claude in the style of different Italian artists each day
- Included in the daily weather email

## Supported formats
- JPG/JPEG
- PNG
- WebP

## Naming suggestion
- `view-1.jpg`, `view-2.jpg`, etc. for different camera angles
- Or use descriptive names like `vineyard.jpg`, `terrace.jpg`, etc.
